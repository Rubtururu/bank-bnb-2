    • Project Description

    BNB Reward Planet is a revolutionary platform where you can stake your BNB holdings to generate daily returns. Our project combines the stability of daily rewards with the thrill of future casino games. Currently featuring an engaging lottery giveaway system, the highest participant during the lottery’s active phase stands a chance to win enticing BNB prizes.

    Participation

    To participate in the project, begin by connecting your wallet through the BNB network. Navigate to the dashboard, where you can specify the amount of BNB you wish to stake (minimum of 0.1 BNB). Proceed by clicking the "Deposit" button and confirming your transaction. Then proceed to your staking portfolio where you can see your active stakes and real time rewards.

    Deposits

    The minimum deposit amount is 0.1 BNB from which a 10% deposit tax will be deducted, with 5% allocated to support the team for project operating expenses, marketing, and development. The remaining 5% serves as a referral reward for the referrer associated with the deposit.

    Withdrawals

    Withdrawals operate under the condition that deposited stakes are locked for a 45 days. After this period, you have the option to fully withdraw your stakes. Alternatively, an emergency withdrawal option is available, enabling you to retrieve 50% of your stake amount, while the remaining 50% is reinvested back into the contract.
    Note: Any form of withdrawal signifies that you are no longer considered active in the project until you decide to make a new deposit.

    Reward System

    The reward system operates with a base rate of 1% daily, escalating by 0.25% every fifth time you compound your stakes. This increase continues until it reaches the maximum rate of 2.5% daily. To ensure contract sustainability, anti-whale measures are implemented. Claiming returns consecutively three times results in a reduction of your rewards percentage by 0.25% each time until it reaches the minimum rate of 1%. This strategy is designed to promote a balanced and prolonged reward structure for our participants.

    Lottery

    The lottery system operates at the team's discretion, allowing activation at any time with a determined duration. The winner is determined by the highest stakes deposited during the lottery's active period. At the end of the lottery period, the winner is eligible for a BNB prize equivalent to 10% of the current contract balance. Stay tuned for exciting opportunities to win substantial rewards during our lottery events!
